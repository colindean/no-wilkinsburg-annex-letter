Things to remember when adding your own comments

Be factual.

Do not lie. Do not exaggerate. Simply state your opposition. Expand on it briefly, but keep it under 100 words.

Re-read anything you have written.

Sending something is better than nothing, but something short and grammatically correct will give the best impression.

Sign your letter.
Your signature means that a person took the time to send this letter. Use a blue pen or some other color for added effect.
